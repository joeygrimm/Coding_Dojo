Getting Started with Woodworking

If you've been interested in taking up woodworking at as a hobby but thought it was too expensive? Well, it's not. You can do a lot with very little. Here is a list of just a few things you'll need to get started.

  • Pencil or Pen
  • Tape Measurer
  • Hand Saw or Electric Circular Saw
  • Hammer
  • Screw Driver
  • Electric Drill
  • Saw Horses
